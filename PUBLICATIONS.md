# Publications Using DRIADA

**DRIADA (Dimensionality Reduction for Integrated Activity Data Analysis)** has been used in the following research publications. The framework's integrated approach to single-neuron selectivity and population dynamics has enabled discoveries across biological and artificial neural systems.

---

## Peer-Reviewed Journal Articles

### Sotskov et al. (2022) — Hippocampal Place Cell Dynamics

**Calcium Imaging Reveals Fast Tuning Dynamics of Hippocampal Place Cells and CA1 Population Activity during Free Exploration Task in Mice**

Sotskov, V.P., Pospelov, N.A., Plusnin, V.V., Anokhin, K.V.

*International Journal of Molecular Sciences*, 23(2), 638 (2022)

DOI: [10.3390/ijms23020638](https://doi.org/10.3390/ijms23020638)

> Used DRIADA for calcium imaging analysis revealing fast tuning dynamics of place cells and CA1 population activity during free exploration.

---

### Pospelov et al. (2021) — Laplacian Eigenmaps for fMRI

**The Laplacian Eigenmaps Dimensionality Reduction of fMRI Data for Discovering Stimulus-Induced Changes in the Resting-State Brain Activity**

Pospelov, N., Tetereva, A., Martynova, O., Anokhin, K.

*NeuroImage: Reports*, 1(3), 100035 (2021)

DOI: [10.1016/j.ynirp.2021.100035](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ynirp.2021.100035)

> Applied DRIADA's Laplacian Eigenmaps implementation to fMRI data for resting-state network investigation.

### Bobyleva et al. (2025) — Structural Connectome Multifractality

**Metric Structural Human Connectomes: Localization and Multifractality of Eigenmodes**

Bobyleva, A., Gorsky, A., Nechaev, S., Valba, O., Pospelov, N.

*Network Neuroscience*, 9(2), 682–711 (2025)

DOI: [10.1162/netn_a_00439](https://doi.org/10.1162/netn_a_00439)

## Citing DRIADA

A paper describing DRIADA has been submitted to the [Journal of Open Source Software (JOSS)](https://joss.theoj.org/). In the meantime, please cite:

```bibtex
@software{driada2026,
  title = {DRIADA: Dimensionality Reduction for Integrated Activity Data},
  author = {Pospelov, Nikita and contributors},
  year = {2026},
  url = {https://github.com/iabs-neuro/driada}
}
```

Consider also citing specific publications above depending on which DRIADA components you use (e.g., cite Sotskov et al. 2022 for calcium imaging applications, Pospelov et al. 2021 for Laplacian Eigenmaps, etc.).
